WHY KINETIC ATMOSFERE

Kinetik Atmosfere is a fitness passion project borne from over 10 years of training experience and learning from the very best in the industry. Our coach envisioned Kinetik Atmosfere as much more than a gym but a dynamic space where expertise meets unbounded zeal! Trained by the best, our coach set out to hand-pick up and coming fitness superstars in their own right. The Kinetik Atmosfere coaching team aren't just super fit and experienced but aspirational individuals who have overcome obstacles to become the warriors they are today. This deep understanding of how transformation occurs makes KinetiX doubly effective. Our coach's mentor, touted as Dubai's best trainer, the legend always says, “Our Bodies are Starving for Motion”. Kinetik Atmosfere is where members come to feed their bodies with the energy needed to truly transform your body, mind and life. We dare our members to incorporate movement and strength in to the core of their life.
